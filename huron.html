Inspired by Lake Huron’s Shoreline

Lake Huron is known for its speckled stones, layered textures, and island‑dotted coastline. These handcrafted rock tacks capture that natural beauty in a small, functional form.

Why Lake Huron Stones?

Speckled & Textured

Lake Huron stones often feature speckles, stripes, and layered mineral patterns. These details make each tack visually interesting and unique.

Balanced Colors

Expect soft grays, warm neutrals, and the occasional surprise pattern. These tones fit beautifully in offices, studios, and natural‑themed spaces.

Calm, Natural Feel

Huron’s shoreline has a peaceful, grounded energy. These tacks bring a small piece of that calm to your workspace.

How They’re Made

1. Stone Selection

Stones are chosen for texture, shape, and personality—small enough for a tack but large enough to show their natural character.

2. Cleaning & Finishing

Each stone is cleaned and lightly finished to highlight its natural patterns without losing its organic feel.

3. Mounting

Stones are securely attached to durable steel thumbtacks using strong adhesive and proper curing time.

4. Set Pairing

Finished tacks are grouped into visually balanced sets—matching tones, textures, and shapes for a cohesive look.
